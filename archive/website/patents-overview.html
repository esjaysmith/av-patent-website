US Patent 12,001,207: End-to-End Neural Network Architecture

Systems and Methods for Camera-Only Autonomous Vehicle Operation

Patent Number: US 12,001,207 B2 Filing Date: March 15, 2023 Grant Date: June 4, 2024 Inventors: Multiple Contributors
Active Available for Licensing

Patent Abstract

A comprehensive system for autonomous vehicle operation using camera-only input through end-to-end neural network processing. The invention encompasses novel architectures for multi-camera data fusion, temporal modeling, and direct vehicle control output generation without traditional modular processing stages.

Key Patent Claims

Claim 1: End-to-End Architecture

Novel neural network architecture that processes raw camera inputs directly to vehicle control commands, eliminating traditional perception-planning-control pipelines.

Claim 8: Multi-Camera Fusion

Advanced algorithms for fusing multiple camera perspectives into unified spatial-temporal representations for autonomous decision-making.

Claim 15: Safety Validation

Integrated safety monitoring and validation systems that ensure reliable operation across diverse driving conditions and edge cases.

Claim 22: Scalable Training

Methodologies for training neural networks on massive datasets from distributed vehicle fleets with continuous learning capabilities.
