# Content Briefs for 50+ Pages

## Content Brief Template

Each content brief includes:
- **Page Title**: SEO-optimized title
- **URL**: Clean, keyword-rich URL
- **Primary Keyword**: Main target keyword
- **Secondary Keywords**: Supporting keywords
- **Meta Description**: 150-160 character description
- **Content Goals**: What the page should achieve
- **Target Audience**: Who the page is for
- **Key Messages**: Main points to communicate
- **Content Structure**: Outline of sections
- **Word Count Target**: Recommended length
- **Visual Elements**: Images, charts, etc.
- **Calls-to-Action**: CTAs to include
- **Internal Links**: Related pages to link to
- **SEO Elements**: Additional optimization

## Content Development Standards

### Quality Requirements
- Original, well-researched content
- Industry expert validation
- Data-driven insights and statistics
- Clear, accessible language
- Actionable recommendations
- Regular updates for freshness

### SEO Optimization
- Primary keyword in title and first paragraph
- Secondary keywords distributed naturally (1-2% density)
- Internal linking to related pages (3-5 links per page)
- Schema markup for rich snippets
- Mobile-optimized formatting
- Fast loading optimization

### Performance Goals
- Target 1,500+ words for landing pages
- Include 3-5 data points or statistics
- Feature 1-2 industry expert quotes
- 2-4 high-quality images or visuals
- Clear calls-to-action throughout
- Related content suggestions

### Editorial Process
- Content briefs approved before writing
- Technical review for accuracy
- SEO optimization review
- Legal review for patent claims
- Final editing and proofreading
- Performance tracking and optimization
