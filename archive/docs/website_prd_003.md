## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Overview
This enhanced PRD provides a detailed, staged implementation approach for developing a comprehensive website with 50+ landing pages focused on generating commercial interest in licensing or purchasing patents related to camera-only end-to-end autonomous driving technology (US 12,001,207 and related applications like 18/432,397).

### 1.2 Development Philosophy
The staged approach ensures:
- Systematic progression from foundation to optimization
- Clear deliverables and success criteria for each stage
- Risk mitigation through iterative development
- Quality assurance at each phase
- Scalable architecture for future enhancements

### 1.3 Key Enhancements from v1
- Detailed task breakdown across 6 development stages
- Specific deliverables and success criteria
- Technical implementation guidelines
- Quality checkpoints and testing protocols
- Performance optimization roadmap

## 2. Objectives (Unchanged from v1)
### 2.1 Primary Objectives
- Generate leads: 100+ inquiries for licensing/purchase within 6 months
- Educate: Communicate patent value, market opportunities, and developments
- SEO Traffic: Rank for keywords like "camera-only autonomous driving patents," "robotaxi licensing 2025"

### 2.2 Secondary Objectives
- Position patents as essential for camera-based AV scalability
- Foster discussions on X/LinkedIn via shareable content
- Track engagement: 10K+ monthly visitors by Month 3
