# User Journey and Conversion Path Mapping

## Target Audiences

1. **OEMs & Automakers** (Primary)
   - Decision makers at Tesla, Ford, GM, etc.
   - R&D and engineering teams
   - Procurement/licensing departments

2. **Tech Companies** (Secondary)
   - AI/ML companies (Nvidia, Mobileye)
   - AV startups and scale-ups
   - Robotics and drone companies

3. **Investors & VCs** (Tertiary)
   - VC firms investing in AV
   - Corporate development teams
   - Strategic investors

4. **Industry Analysts** (Influencers)
   - Research firms, analysts
   - Journalists covering AV industry

## User Journey Stages

### Stage 1: Awareness (Homepage)
**Goal**: Capture attention and establish credibility

**User Actions**:
- Visit homepage from search/social
- Read hero section value proposition
- Scan key benefits and statistics
- View trust indicators (patent numbers, industry endorsements)

**Conversion Points**:
- Newsletter signup
- Download whitepaper
- Watch explainer video

**Content Elements**:
- Hero: "License Camera-Only Autonomous Driving Patents"
- Key stats: "$124B robotaxi market", "50-70% cost savings"
- Social proof: Industry quotes, patent badges
- CTAs: "Learn More", "Contact Us", "Download Patent Brief"

### Stage 2: Interest (Core Pages)
**Goal**: Build understanding and demonstrate value

**User Actions**:
- Navigate to Patents Overview
- Read Technology Benefits
- Explore Market Analysis
- Review Case Studies

**Journey Paths**:
1. **Technical Path**: Homepage → Patents Overview → Technology Benefits → Licensing Info
2. **Business Path**: Homepage → Market Analysis → Case Studies → Licensing Info
3. **Industry Path**: Homepage → About → Case Studies → Contact

**Conversion Points**:
- Form submissions for more information
- Document downloads
- Webinar registrations

### Stage 3: Consideration (Landing Pages)
**Goal**: Address specific concerns and objections

**User Actions**:
- Click from core pages to relevant landing pages
- Read industry-specific content
- Compare options and ROI calculations
- Review competitor analysis

**Journey Paths**:
1. **Tesla Focus**: Patents Overview → Tesla FSD pages → Licensing ROI → Contact
2. **Market Focus**: Market Analysis → Growth forecasts → VC Funding → Contact
3. **Industry Focus**: Technology Benefits → Industry endorsements → OEM Economics → Contact

**Conversion Points**:
- Qualified lead forms
- Consultation requests
- Partnership inquiries

### Stage 4: Decision (Contact/Licensing)
**Goal**: Close the deal with detailed information

**User Actions**:
- Review licensing process
- Access detailed patent documentation
- Request custom proposals
- Schedule meetings

**Conversion Points**:
- Licensing agreement initiations
- Partnership discussions
- Investment inquiries
