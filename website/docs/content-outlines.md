## Content Development Guidelines

### Word Count Targets
- Homepage: 800-1,000 words
- Core Pages: 1,200-1,800 words
- Landing Pages: 1,500-2,200 words

### Content Structure
- SEO-optimized H1 title
- Compelling introduction (200-300 words)
- Main content sections with H2s
- Data and statistics integration
- Industry quotes and endorsements
- Patent tie-ins throughout
- Clear calls-to-action
- Related content suggestions

### SEO Optimization
- Primary keyword in title and first paragraph
- Secondary keywords distributed naturally
- Internal linking to related pages
- Schema markup for rich snippets
- Mobile-optimized formatting
- Fast loading optimization

### Quality Standards
- Original, well-researched content
- Industry expert validation
- Data-driven insights
- Clear, jargon-free explanations
- Actionable recommendations
- Regular content updates
