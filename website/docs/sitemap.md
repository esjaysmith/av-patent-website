## URL Structure Guidelines

- Homepage: `/`
- Core pages: `/{page-name}` (kebab-case)
- Landing pages: `/{topic}-{focus}-{aspect}` (kebab-case, descriptive)
- All URLs lowercase, no special characters
- Maximum 50 characters per URL
- SEO-friendly with target keywords included

## Navigation Structure

- Main navigation: Home, Patents, Market, Technology, Licensing, About, Contact
- Footer: Resources, FAQ, Case Studies, Contact
- Breadcrumbs on all pages
- Related links sections on landing pages

## Internal Linking Strategy

- Homepage links to all core pages
- Core pages link to relevant landing pages
- Landing pages link to licensing/contact pages
- Cross-linking between related topics
- Footer links to important pages
- CTA buttons throughout content
