## Content Production Workflow

### Pre-Production (1-2 days per page)
1. **Keyword Research Validation** - Confirm search volume and competition
2. **Competitor Content Analysis** - Identify gaps and opportunities
3. **Content Brief Finalization** - Approve structure and requirements
4. **Resource Gathering** - Collect data, quotes, images
5. **Technical Review Planning** - Schedule expert validation

### Production (2-3 days per page)
1. **First Draft Writing** - Complete initial content creation
2. **SEO Optimization** - Integrate keywords naturally
3. **Fact Checking** - Verify all data and claims
4. **Image Selection** - Choose and optimize visuals
5. **Internal Linking** - Add relevant cross-references

### Post-Production (1-2 days per page)
1. **Technical Review** - Subject matter expert validation
2. **SEO Review** - Keyword optimization and structure check
3. **Legal Review** - Patent claim and content accuracy
4. **Editing and Proofreading** - Grammar, style, clarity
5. **Final Optimization** - Meta tags, schema markup, formatting

### Publishing and Promotion (0.5 days per page)
1. **Quality Assurance Testing** - Links, forms, mobile display
2. **Publishing and Deployment** - Add to CMS and publish
3. **Indexation Setup** - Submit to search engines
4. **Social Media Promotion** - Share on relevant platforms
5. **Email Newsletter** - Announce to subscriber list
