Patent Licensing Basics

What patents are included in your portfolio?

Our portfolio includes US Patent 12,001,207 covering end-to-end neural network architectures for camera-only autonomous driving, plus 15+ related applications covering camera processing, safety systems, and training methodologies.

How long does the licensing process take?

The typical licensing process takes 4-8 weeks from initial contact to signed agreement, depending on the complexity of your implementation and review requirements.

What geographic regions are covered?

Our patents provide coverage across major automotive markets including the United States, European Union, China, Japan, and South Korea.

Yes, our patents cover integration methodologies for various vehicle platforms. We provide specific guidance for adaptation to different automotive architectures.
